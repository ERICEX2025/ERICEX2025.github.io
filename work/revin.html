Revin

Jun 2024 – Aug 2024 Software Engineer Intern

Developed AI agents at a $10M early-stage startup, automating front-office workflows for home service businesses. I worked on improving AI-powered SMS scheduling agents, securing authentication systems, and building scalable infrastructure to support the company's growth.

AI Agent Performance Optimization

Boosted booking rates from 16% to 46% across 18,667 conversations, generating an additional 4,257 bookings by re-engineering our AI-powered SMS scheduling agent for Sears. This performance improvement outperformed the conversion rate of live human agents, demonstrating the effectiveness of well-designed AI systems.

Security & Authentication

Identified a severe login bypass vulnerability in local storage and integrated Supabase Auth to secure authentication. This security improvement protected user data and ensured proper access control across the application.

Analytics & Monitoring

Implemented fail-reason and intervention-needed tagging for our agent dashboards, improving analytics and timely escalation. This enhancement provided better visibility into agent performance and enabled proactive support when needed.

Infrastructure & Scalability

Led efforts to consolidate client-specific repos and built an extensive logging system, enhancing scalability and sustainability. This infrastructure work improved code maintainability and provided better observability for the engineering team.

Reflection

Working at Revin was an incredible experience that taught me how to build production-ready AI systems at scale. The opportunity to work on real-world problems, from optimizing AI agent performance to securing authentication systems, gave me valuable insights into what it takes to build and maintain software in a fast-paced startup environment. Seeing the direct impact of my work through improved booking rates and enhanced security was incredibly rewarding.
